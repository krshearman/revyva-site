Ebook Table of Contents

  1. Deciding What Path to Take

    Finding clarity in your next chapter so you can move forward with confidence

    Your career change starts with a clear vision. This chapter helps you assess your strengths, values, and priorities while exploring career options that align with your lifestyle and long-term goals. You’ll learn proven exercises for narrowing down choices so your pivot feels exciting—not overwhelming.

  2. Translating Your Career Skills for Today’s Market

    Turning your experience into currency employers can’t resist

    You already have valuable skills—now it’s time to reframe them for the modern job market. Learn how to identify your most transferable abilities, highlight them in job applications, and position yourself as a strong candidate even in industries you’ve never worked in before.

  3. To School or Not to School

    When more education is a wise investment—and when it’s just a distraction

    Is going back to school really the key to a better career, or will it drain your time and money? This chapter gives you a framework for deciding if additional training, certifications, or degrees will actually pay off, and introduces alternative learning options that fit your schedule and budget.

  4. Building a Brand That Opens Doors

    Crafting a professional identity that makes you impossible to ignore

    Your personal brand is more than a résumé—it’s the story you tell about who you are and what you bring to the table. Here you’ll learn how to update your résumé, LinkedIn, and professional presence to reflect your new direction and attract the right opportunities.

  5. Networking in the New Era

    Making authentic connections that lead to real opportunities

    Networking doesn’t have to feel fake or forced. Discover strategies for building genuine relationships—both in person and online—that lead to meaningful introductions, insider information, and job offers.

  6. Navigating Ageism and Bias with Confidence

    Flipping the script and turning your age into your biggest asset

    Yes, bias exists—but you have tools to counter it. Learn how to address subtle ageism, emphasize your unique value, and use your experience as a strength instead of something to hide.

  7. The Modern Job Hunt Toolbox

    Mastering the tech, tactics, and tools to land your next role faster

    From applicant tracking systems to AI-powered résumé scanning, the hiring process has changed. This chapter gives you the digital know-how, application strategies, and interview techniques you need to stand out in a crowded field.

  8. Financial Planning for a Career Transition

    Creating a safety net so you can leap without fear

    A career pivot can come with income gaps or temporary instability. Learn how to budget, save, and plan for a smoother transition so you can pursue your next step without constant financial stress.

  9. Side Hustles, Freelancing, and Entrepreneurial Paths

    Building income streams that fit your life and your dreams

    Your next chapter doesn’t have to be a traditional 9-to-5. Explore ways to start a side hustle, freelance business, or entrepreneurial venture that leverages your skills while offering flexibility and independence.

  10. Thriving in Your Second Act

    Staying relevant, resilient, and fulfilled in your new career

    Landing the job or launching the business is just the beginning. This chapter covers how to maintain momentum, keep your skills fresh, and continue growing so your second act becomes your best act yet.
